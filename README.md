<h2>The Power of Empathy</h2>
<p>Sometimes people do not need advice,solutions nor lectures. What they truly need is someone who understands how they feel.</p>

<p>Empathy is quiet to the ability to step into another person's world. It's not more of “I’m sorry for you.” but “I understand you.”</p>

<p>In relationships, friendships, and even with strangers, empathy builds trust and connection. It reminds people that they are not alone in their struggles.</p>

<p>The world becomes softer when we choose empathy over judgment. Sometimes the greatest gift we can give someone is simply understanding their feelings.</p>
